about

hey! i'm madeleine wang, a data engineer and ml researcher based in southern california. i work at capital group as a data engineer and i'm pursuing an m.s. in statistics (data science) at stanford. i studied data science at uc berkeley (regents' & chancellor's scholar).

my work sits at the intersection of developer experience and ai. i care deeply about how ai systems are designed so that humans can trust, understand, and safely rely on them. at capital group i've built ai-assisted developer workflows and authored a technical critique of our org-wide ai skill architecture that was adopted as the basis for a full redesign. getting that right (the ergonomics, the transparency, the guardrails) is what i find most interesting about building with ai.

i'm particularly drawn to questions about how ai tools shape the behavior and judgment of the people who use them, and what good human-ai collaboration looks like in high-stakes environments. i think carefully-designed developer experience is an underrated lever for alignment: if a system makes the safe, correct action the easy one, you've already done a lot of the work.
