about

hey! i'm madeleine wang, a data engineer based in southern california. i'm pursuing an m.s. in statistics (data science) at stanford and i studied data science at uc berkeley.

my work sits at the intersection of developer experience and ai. i care deeply about how ai systems are designed so that humans can trust, understand, and safely rely on them. getting that right (the ergonomics, the transparency, the guardrails) is what i find most interesting about building with ai.

i'm particularly drawn to questions about how ai tools shape the behavior and judgment of the people who use them, and what good human-ai collaboration looks like in high-stakes environments. i am constantly thinking about developer experience, using that a benchmark for alignment: if a system makes the safe, correct action the easy one, you've already done a lot of the work.
